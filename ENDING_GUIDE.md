# Ending Guide

Use this to test each ending. All steps assume a fresh run unless noted.

## Ritual Bargain (performed_ritual)
1) Entry Hall: pick up the old key from the coat rack.
2) Hallway: unlock the fusuma (requires old key) to reach Storage Closet.
3) Storage Closet: take the ritual talisman.
4) Outside Porch: requires car keys to go outside (see Escape routes) OR take the Study shortcut.
5) Study: read the diary in Grandmother's Room to unlock the shrine shortcut.
6) Study: use the shortcut to Hidden Shrine.
7) Hidden Shrine: use the altar with the ritual talisman.

## True Escape (found_car_keys + found_shrine_map + got_outside, sanity 40-100)
1) Living Room: take car keys from sofa.
2) Study: open the locked drawer with the old key to get the shrine map.
3) Entry Hall: go to Outside Porch (requires found_car_keys).
4) Outside Porch: interact with the car (requires shrine map) to set got_outside.
5) Keep sanity at 40 or above by avoiding heavy sanity-cost events.

## Cosmic Truth (read_forbidden_book + learned_covenant + seen_entity_origin)
1) Grandmother's Room: inspect the butsudan to reveal the turned photo.
2) Grandmother's Room: inspect the turned photo to set seen_entity_origin.
3) Study: inspect the desk to set learned_covenant.
4) Study: inspect the strange book to set read_forbidden_book.

## Consumed (sanity = 0)
1) Trigger high sanity costs: strange book, bathtub event, mirror, TV, diary, desk, and ambient scares.
2) Keep interacting until sanity hits 0.

## Escaped, But... (sanity 1-30 + found_car_keys, NOT read_forbidden_book)
1) Living Room: take car keys.
2) Drop sanity below 30 without reading the strange book.
3) End the run once the ending screen appears.

## Sealed In (sanity 1-20 + sealed_hallway, NOT got_outside)
1) Storage Closet: take salt pouch (sets found_salt_pouch).
2) Hallway: use salt line (requires salt pouch) to set sealed_hallway.
3) Drop sanity to 1-20 without going outside.

## Dawn (sanity 60-100 + found_car_keys + survived_until_dawn)
1) Living Room: take car keys.
2) Keep sanity above 60.
3) Wait until time reaches 100 by interacting and navigating (time advances per action).
